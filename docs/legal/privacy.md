# Privacy Policy

**Last updated: 2026-07-03**

## Overview

This privacy policy applies to the Kizuna documentation website at [use-kizuna.com](https://use-kizuna.com).

Kizuna is open-source, self-hosted communication software. The Kizuna project does not operate any hosted chat service. This policy only covers this documentation website.

## Data We Collect

**We do not collect any personal data.** This website:

- Does not use cookies
- Does not use analytics or tracking of any kind
- Does not collect email addresses, names, or any other personal information
- Does not have user accounts or login

Your browser may store your preferred color theme (light/dark mode) locally on your device using [localStorage](https://developer.mozilla.org/en-US/docs/Web/API/Window/localStorage). This data never leaves your device and is not accessible to us.

## Hosting

This website is hosted by Vercel Inc. As part of providing their hosting service, Vercel may process standard server log data (such as IP addresses, browser type, and timestamps). Please refer to [Vercel's Privacy Policy](https://vercel.com/legal/privacy-policy) for details on how they handle this data.

## Kizuna Software

Kizuna is **self-hosted** software. When you join or operate a Kizuna server:

- The **server operator** is the data controller for any data processed on that server (messages, voice data, uploaded files, account information).
- The Kizuna project has **no access** to data on any self-hosted instance.
- If you self-host Kizuna, you are responsible for your own privacy policy and for complying with applicable data protection laws, including informing your users about what data you collect and how you process it.

## Your Rights Under GDPR

If you are in the European Union, you have rights under the General Data Protection Regulation regarding your personal data. Since we do not collect any personal data through this website, there is no personal data to exercise these rights over.

For data held on a specific self-hosted Kizuna server, contact the operator of that server.

## Third-Party Links

This website may link to third-party websites (such as GitHub). We are not responsible for the privacy practices of those websites.

## Changes

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.
