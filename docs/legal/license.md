## Why AGPLv3?

The AGPLv3 was chosen to ensure that all improvements to Kizuna remain free and open for everyone. If someone modifies Kizuna and offers it as a service, the AGPLv3 ensures their users also benefit from those modifications.

This is a stronger copyleft than the GPL or MIT — it closes the "ASP loophole" where companies could modify GPL software, run it as a service, and never release their changes.
